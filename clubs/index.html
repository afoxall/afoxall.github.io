Queen's Engineering has innovative classes and countless ways for students to get involved.

Read on to learn about what I'm doing in and out of the classroom.

Queen's Technology and Media Association

QTMA is a product management club that helps students experience real tech projects in an entrepreneurial environemnt. I am a senior developer, which means I will lead the development of one of the projects - an online journal which will use NLP to analyze and track users' mental state.

EngLinks

EngLinks is a student-run tutoring service at Queen's Engineering. I love to teach, whether it is tutoring, TAing, or just helping classmates. I tutor several courses including data structures, discrete math and differential equations.

Queen's Mostly Autonomous Saiboat Team (MAST) 2015-2017

MAST is a student-run design team that, as the name suggests, builds mostly autonomous sailboats for competition.

I was a co-manager of the software team, the group that writes code to control the boat. Most of the existing code was written by a student a couple years ago, so I got pretty good at navigating poorly styled and documented code.
