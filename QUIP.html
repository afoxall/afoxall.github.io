Queen's Undergraduate Internship Program (QUIP) is Queens' version of a co-op program, but instead of rotating 4-month terms students can take a whole year off and work for 12-16 months. The idea behind this difference is that it can take a month or two for students to get caught up at a new company. With a 4-month term this is a huge portion of the internship, so a longer internship reduces the relative impact of this learning period. For more information view the QUIP Employer's Guide.
